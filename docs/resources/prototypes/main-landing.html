Frequently asked questions

Short answers to the stuff people ask before they commit to a daily routine.

Is this a medical app?

No. It tracks your routine and self‑reported signals. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice.

Do I need a trainer?

No. Trainer oversight is optional — useful when someone adjusts plans or reviews reports.

What do I track each day?

Today’s session completion + a quick check‑in (sleep, nutrition, fatigue). That’s it.

What if I miss a day?

Nothing breaks. The goal is a sustainable streak, not perfection.
